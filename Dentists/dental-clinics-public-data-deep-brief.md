# Public Data Sources for Dental Clinics: Comprehensive Inventory

**Research Date:** February 11, 2026
**Purpose:** Complete inventory of publicly accessible data sources about dental clinics with actionable insights for value creation

---

## Executive Summary

This research identifies **9 major categories** of public data sources for dental clinics, covering 50+ specific data points and 30+ tools. Key finding: **78% of local SEO impact** comes from just three signals (GBP optimization, backlinks, reviews), making these the highest-value targets for data-driven insights.

**Market Context (2026):**
- Global dental market: $49.58B → $135.73B by 2035 (11.84% CAGR)
- U.S. dental services: $185.39B (2026) → $270.57B by 2034
- DSOs now represent 39% of U.S. practices (up from 23% in 2022)
- 68% of dental searches occur on smartphones

- Team photos
- Patient photos (with consent)

**Reviews & Ratings:**
- Overall star rating (1-5 scale)
- Total review count
- Review distribution by rating
- Review text content and sentiment
- Review response rate and quality
- Review velocity (reviews/month trend)
- Reviewer profiles and history
- **Critical:** Direct ranking factor for local SEO

**Q&A Section:**
- Questions asked by potential patients
- Answers provided by business or community
- Question frequency and topics
- Response time metrics

**Posts & Updates:**
- Promotional posts
- Event announcements
- Product/service highlights
- COVID-19 updates
- Post engagement metrics

**Performance Metrics (via GBP Insights):**
- How customers find listing (direct vs. discovery searches)
- Customer actions (calls, website visits, direction requests)
- Photo views
- Search queries used to find business
- Geographic data on searchers

**2026 Updates:**
- AI-powered messaging capabilities
- Advanced booking integrations
- Real-time data syncing
- Enhanced verification protocols (anti-spam)
- Increased weight on user-generated content in rankings

**Free Tools:**

- All active ads from any advertiser
- Ad creative (images, videos, text)
- Ad format type
- Platforms running on (Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp, Messenger, Audience Network)
- Start date of ad
- Geographic targeting (regions)

- Historical ads (for issues/elections/politics)

**Access:**
- Completely free
- No account required
- Searchable by: keyword, page name, advertiser name
- URL: facebook.com/ads/library

**Limitations:**
- Does NOT show ad performance (clicks, conversions)
- Does NOT show detailed targeting criteria
- Does NOT show budget/spend (except political ads)
- Limited demographic data

## Key Insights & Contradictions

### Validated Findings

✅ **GBP Optimization = #1 Priority:** Consistently cited as top local ranking factor
✅ **Mobile-First Critical:** 68% of dental searches on mobile (2026)
✅ **Reviews Drive Rankings:** Direct correlation between review volume and local pack position
✅ **Citation Impact Declining:** Down to 7-10% from 15-20% (still valuable strategically)
✅ **Social Media Influences Decisions:** 41% of patients cite social media as decision factor
✅ **Speed Matters:** 70%+ of dental sites fail Core Web Vitals

### Areas of Nuance

⚠️ **Traffic Estimation Accuracy:** SimilarWeb/Ahrefs traffic estimates can vary 30-50% from actual
⚠️ **Review Platform Priorities:** Google > Yelp > Healthgrades varies by demographic
⚠️ **Social Platform ROI:** Instagram strong for cosmetics, Facebook for family dentistry
⚠️ **Tech Stack Detection:** Not always 100% accurate, especially with custom implementations

### Data Gaps & Limitations

❌ **Exact Patient Volume:** Not directly observable, must be inferred from proxies
❌ **Revenue Data:** Private practices don't disclose financials
❌ **Ad Performance Metrics:** Meta/Google transparency centers don't show conversions
❌ **Private Practice Management Systems:** Internal operations data not accessible
