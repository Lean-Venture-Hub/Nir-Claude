## Direct Competitors (Holon / South Tel Aviv)

### 1. DI Smile Dental — Same Azrieli Building
- **Location**: Azrieli Center Holon (literally next door)
- **Advantage**: Active Google profile, regular posts, better online visibility
- **Weakness**: No proprietary technology, no academic credentials
- **Threat Level**: HIGH (same building, different visibility)

### 2. Dr. Itai Laser Dental
- **Location**: Holon area
- **Reviews**: 100+ Google reviews
- **Advantage**: Strong SEO, runs paid ads
- **Weakness**: General dentist, not a specialist surgeon
- **Threat Level**: HIGH (dominating local search)

### 3. Dental Clinics via Health Funds (Leumit, Maccabi, Clalit)
- **Advantage**: Built-in patient flow, low cost
- **Weakness**: Long waits, less personal, no complex procedures
- **Threat Level**: MEDIUM (different patient segment)

### 4. Premium Tel Aviv Clinics
- **Examples**: Dr. Shahar Dray (Park Tzameret), Dentist Tel Aviv, MEDART Ramat Aviv
- **Advantage**: Tel Aviv prestige, strong digital, higher prices
- **Weakness**: Higher prices, parking/access issues
- **Threat Level**: MEDIUM (patients may prefer Holon convenience + equivalent quality)
