## Key Insights for Value Creation

### Massive Digital Gap
- **74.6K followers** for #1 (Smile Clinic) — low ceiling for the industry
- Most chains have **zero social media** presence
- 55-65% of clinics have **no digital presence beyond a basic listing**
- Instagram is severely underutilized despite dentistry being highly visual

### Market Structure Creates Opportunity
- 75-80% independent = fragmented, unoptimized, no marketing support
- Health funds control clinics but don't optimize digital presence
- 4 chains have **failed recently** — surviving ones need differentiation
- No significant DSO/PE consolidation (unlike US at 39%)

### Israel-Specific Angles
- Dental tourism = $60M+ opportunity but digital marketing lags behind Turkey/Thailand
- 22.5% of out-of-pocket healthcare spending goes to dental
- Children covered universally, adults largely out-of-pocket
- High competition in Tel Aviv/Jerusalem, lower in periphery
