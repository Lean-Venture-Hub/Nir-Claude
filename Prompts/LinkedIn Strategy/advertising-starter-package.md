## Tier 1: $1,500 Budget (Essentials)

**Best For**: Testing LinkedIn ads for the first time, validating ICP, single product/service focus

### Campaign Structure

**Campaign 1: Lead Generation (100% of budget)**
- **Budget**: $1,500 total ($50/day for 30 days)
- **Objective**: Lead Generation (native LinkedIn lead forms)
- **Ad Format**: Single Image Ads
- **Creative**: 3 ad variations testing different value propositions

### Targeting Strategy

**Single ICP Focus**:
- 1 primary persona (e.g., "Marketing Directors at B2B SaaS companies")
- Geographic: 1-2 regions (US, UK, or primary market)
- Company size: 50-500 employees (tight range)
- **Estimated Audience**: 50K-150K

**Targeting Criteria**:
- Job titles: 5-8 specific titles
- Seniority: Manager, Director, VP levels
- Industries: 2-3 relevant industries
- No job function/skills targeting (too broad for small budget)

### Ad Creative Approach

**3 Single Image Ads**:
1. **Problem-focused**: Lead with pain point
2. **Outcome-focused**: Lead with benefit/result
3. **Social proof**: Lead with customer stat/testimonial

**Creative Specs**:
- Format: 1200√ó627 px (landscape) single images
- Copy: Primary text (150 chars), Headline (70 chars), CTA
- Testing variable: Hook/angle (keep visuals consistent)

### Budget Allocation

- **Daily Budget**: $50/day
- **Bid Strategy**: Maximum delivery (let LinkedIn optimize)
- **Duration**: 30 days
- **Expected Spend**: $1,500 ¬± $50

### Success Metrics (30 Days)

**Primary KPIs**:
- **Leads**: 15-30 leads (target CPL: $50-100)
- **CTR**: 0.4%-0.8% (benchmark: 0.5%)
- **Conversion Rate**: 8%-15% (lead form submission)

**Learning Outcomes**:
- Which value proposition resonates best
- Actual CPL for this ICP
- Lead quality assessment (% sales-qualified)

### Optimization Timeline

**Week 1**: Let campaigns run, gather data (no changes)
**Week 2**: Pause worst-performing ad, increase budget on winner
**Week 3**: Refine audience (exclude low-performers)
**Week 4**: Double down on winning combination

## Campaign Setup Details (All Tiers)

### LinkedIn Campaign Manager Configuration

**Campaign Group (All Tiers)**:
- Create one Campaign Group per budget tier
- Name: "[Company] - Starter Package - [Month Year]"
- Set total campaign group budget (acts as safety cap)

**Individual Campaigns**:
- Name clearly: "[Objective] - [ICP] - [Ad Format]"
- Example: "Lead Gen - Marketing Directors - Single Image"

**Ad Sets (Within Each Campaign)**:
- Split by ICP or testing variable
- Example: Ad Set 1 = ICP 1, Ad Set 2 = ICP 2

**Bidding Strategy Recommendations**:

**Tier 1 ($1.5K)**:
- Use **Maximum Delivery** (automated bidding)
- Let LinkedIn optimize for lowest cost per result

**Tier 2 ($3K)**:
- Start with **Maximum Delivery** for first 2 weeks
- Switch to **Target Cost** once CPL stabilizes (set target = baseline CPL)

**Tier 3 ($6K)**:
- **Maximum Delivery** for cold prospecting campaigns
- **Target Cost** for retargeting (set aggressive target)
- **Manual Bidding** for ABM (bid 20-30% above suggested)

### Audience Sizing Guidelines

**Optimal Audience Size by Budget**:
- **$1.5K**: 50K-150K (narrow targeting)
- **$3K**: 100K-300K total across campaigns
- **$6K**: 300K-800K total across campaigns

**Red Flags**:
- ‚ùå Audience < 20K: Too small, will exhaust quickly, high CPMs
- ‚ùå Audience > 1M: Too broad, wasted spend, low relevance
- ‚úÖ Sweet spot: 50K-200K per ad set for $30-50/day spend

### Ad Format Selection by Objective

**Lead Generation Objective**:
- ‚úÖ Single Image Ads (proven performer)
- ‚úÖ Video Ads (if you have quality video assets)
- ‚ùå Carousel Ads (lower conversion rates for lead gen)
- ‚ùå Text Ads (low visibility, poor performance)

**Awareness/Engagement Objective**:
- ‚úÖ Video Ads (highest engagement)
- ‚úÖ Carousel Ads (storytelling, educational content)
- ‚úÖ Single Image Ads (static thought leadership)

**Website Conversions**:
- ‚úÖ Single Image Ads
- ‚úÖ Video Ads
- ‚ö†Ô∏è Requires strong conversion tracking setup

### Lead Form Best Practices

**Form Length**:
- **Tier 1 ($1.5K)**: Use 3-field forms (Name, Email, Company)
  - Goal: Maximize volume, qualify later

- **Tier 2 ($3K)**: Use 4-5 field forms (+ Job Title, Phone)
  - Goal: Balance volume with quality

- **Tier 3 ($6K)**:
  - Cold campaigns: 3-4 fields
  - Retargeting: 5-6 fields (qualify harder)
  - ABM: 6-7 fields (custom questions)

**Form Fields to Include**:
- ‚úÖ Required: First Name, Last Name, Email, Company Name
- ‚úÖ Recommended: Job Title, Phone (optional), Company Size
- ‚ö†Ô∏è Custom Questions: Use 1-2 max (e.g., "What's your biggest challenge?")

**Privacy Policy**:
- Must include link to privacy policy
- Pre-check consent boxes where legally allowed

1. Install on all website pages

3. Takes 24-48 hours to start showing data

**Conversion Actions** (set up in Campaign Manager):
- ‚úÖ Lead form submission (auto-tracked)

- ‚úÖ Demo request (track via URL)

**UTM Parameters** (consistent tagging):
- Source: `utm_source=linkedin`
- Medium: `utm_medium=paid_social`
- Campaign: `utm_campaign=[campaign_name]`

**Recommended Integrations**:
- HubSpot (native integration via Campaign Manager)

**Why This Matters**:
- See which campaigns generate sales, not just leads

## Optimization Framework

### Week-by-Week Actions

**Week 1: Observation**
- ‚ùå Don't change anything (need data to stabilize)
- ‚úÖ Monitor daily: spend pacing, impressions, CTR
- ‚úÖ Check: Are campaigns delivering? Any errors?

**Week 2: First Optimization**
- ‚úÖ Pause ads with CTR < 0.3% (if spend > $100)
- ‚úÖ Pause audiences with CPL > 2x target
- ‚úÖ Increase budget on best-performing ad by 20%

**Week 3: Refinement**
- ‚úÖ Add negative exclusions (companies, job titles not converting)
- ‚úÖ Test 1-2 new ad variations based on winners
- ‚úÖ Adjust bid strategy if consistently under/over-spending

**Week 4+: Scale**
- ‚úÖ Increase budget on winners by 30-50%
- ‚úÖ Duplicate winning campaigns with slight variations
- ‚úÖ Expand targeting modestly (lookalike, new titles)

### Red Flags (When to Pause/Adjust)

**Pause Campaign If**:
- CPL > 3x your target AND spend > $300
- CTR < 0.2% after $200 spend
- No conversions after $500 spend
- Audience exhausted (frequency > 3, declining performance)

**Adjust Targeting If**:
- Impressions too low (< 1,000/day with $50/day budget) ‚Üí Broaden
- CTR good but no conversions ‚Üí Audience quality issue, tighten targeting
- High frequency (> 2.5) ‚Üí Expand audience or pause ad

**Creative Refresh If**:
- CTR declining week-over-week (ad fatigue)
- Frequency > 3 and performance dropping
- After 30 days, regardless of performance (proactive refresh)

## Post-Campaign Analysis

### Data to Collect (All Tiers)

**Campaign Performance**:
- Total spend by campaign/ad set
- Impressions, clicks, CTR
- Leads generated, conversion rate, CPL
- Best/worst performing ads (by CTR and CPL)

**Audience Performance**:
- Best/worst ICPs (by CPL and lead quality)
- Job title performance (which titles converted best)
- Company size/industry insights
- Geographic performance (if targeting multiple regions)

**Creative Performance**:
- Ad format performance (single image vs video vs carousel)
- Hook/angle performance (problem vs outcome vs social proof)
- Visual style performance (if tested)

**Lead Quality**:
- SQL (Sales Qualified Lead) rate by campaign
- Opportunity creation rate
- Deal close rate (if enough time elapsed)
- Average deal size from LinkedIn leads

### Recommendations for Next Phase

**If Performance Was Good** (met targets):
- ‚úÖ Increase budget by 50-100%
- ‚úÖ Expand to additional ICPs (1-2 new personas)
- ‚úÖ Test new ad formats (if not tried yet)
- ‚úÖ Launch retargeting campaign (if not in current tier)
- ‚úÖ Scale winning campaigns aggressively

**If Performance Was Mixed** (some winners, some losers):
- ‚úÖ Double down on winners only
- ‚úÖ Pause underperformers completely
- ‚úÖ Refine ICP targeting (tighter criteria)
- ‚úÖ Creative refresh (test new angles)
- ‚úÖ Maintain same budget until optimization complete

**If Performance Was Poor** (missed targets significantly):
- ‚ö†Ô∏è Diagnose root cause:
  - Targeting issue? (low CTR, wrong audience)
  - Creative issue? (low CTR, weak messaging)
  - Offer issue? (high CTR, low conversion)

- ‚úÖ Fix identified issue before scaling
- ‚úÖ Consider testing different campaign objective
- ‚úÖ Reduce budget or pause until issues resolved

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

### Targeting Mistakes

‚ùå **Targeting too broad**: "All Marketing professionals" (1M+ audience)
‚úÖ **Fix**: Narrow to specific job titles + seniority + company size

‚ùå **Targeting too narrow**: "CMOs at Series B SaaS in SF" (2K audience)
‚úÖ **Fix**: Expand geography or add adjacent titles

‚ùå **Using too many filters**: Job title + Skills + Groups + Interests
‚úÖ **Fix**: Use 2-3 filters max (title + company size + industry)

### Budget Mistakes

‚ùå **Daily budget too low**: $10/day (won't deliver, high CPMs)
‚úÖ **Fix**: Minimum $25/day per campaign, ideally $50+

‚ùå **Campaign ends mid-month**: Budget exhausted early
‚úÖ **Fix**: Set campaign group budget cap, monitor pacing weekly

‚ùå **Spreading budget too thin**: 6 campaigns at $10/day each
‚úÖ **Fix**: 2-3 campaigns at $25-50/day each

### Creative Mistakes

‚ùå **Too much text on image**: Walls of text, tiny font
‚úÖ **Fix**: Minimal text on image, large headlines, primary text does heavy lifting

‚ùå **No clear CTA**: Image with no direction
‚úÖ **Fix**: Strong, specific CTA (not "Learn More"‚Äîuse "Get the Guide" "Book a Demo")

‚ùå **Generic stock photos**: Handshakes, people pointing at laptops
‚úÖ **Fix**: Real product screenshots, authentic team photos, or bold graphics

### Optimization Mistakes

‚ùå **Changing too much too fast**: Tweaking daily
‚úÖ **Fix**: Wait 1 week minimum before first changes

‚ùå **Never refreshing creative**: Running same ads for 60+ days
‚úÖ **Fix**: Refresh creative every 30 days, proactively

‚ùå **Ignoring lead quality**: Optimizing for cheapest CPL only
‚úÖ **Fix**: Track SQL rate, optimize for quality not just volume
