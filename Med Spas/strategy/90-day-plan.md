## Top 3 Priorities

### 1. Build the Retention System playbook (Days 1-14)
Before outreach, the product must exist. Build and document:
- Win-back email/SMS sequence (3-touch, 90-day cadence) for Vagaro, Boulevard, Zenoti
- Post-visit review request trigger logic
- GBP monthly update checklist
- Onboarding checklist for new clients
- 1-page offer sheet (PDF) for use in sales calls

**Owner:** You. **Deadline:** Day 14. **Gate:** Do not start outreach until playbook is done.

### 2. Run outreach to DFW Segment 1 (Days 15-60)
- Filter labeled-medspas.csv: segment=1, practice_type=Group, review_recency=Fresh, city=DFW
- Find owner contact (website, LinkedIn, Instagram bio)
- 30 cold emails/day + 10 IG DMs/day
- 3 email variants, A/B test subject lines (track open rate, reply rate)
- Goal: 5 discovery calls booked by Day 45, 3 closed by Day 60

### 3. Close and deliver first 3 clients (Days 45-90)
- Discovery call = 20-min diagnostic using their data (review count, site score, booking platform)
- Close on the call or within 48 hrs — no proposals, just the 1-page offer sheet
- Collect $3,000 setup + $1,500 Month 1 upfront
- Onboard within 5 business days of signing
- Track: rebookings attributed, review count before/after, GBP stats

## Risks

| Risk | Likelihood | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|------------|
| Cold email ignored | Medium | Data-personalized openers using their specific review count + site score |
| Platform integration scope creep | Medium | Build Vagaro first. Boulevard in Month 2. Zenoti in Month 3. |
| Dentist proof doesn't transfer | Low | Frame as "appointment-based patient retention" — universal, not vertical-specific |
| Owner too DIY to buy | Medium | Qualify in 2 questions: "What's your 90-day rebook rate?" They won't know. |
| Pricing friction | Low | Anchor on 1 rebooked Botox patient = $400-700. They pay $1,500/mo. Math is obvious. |
