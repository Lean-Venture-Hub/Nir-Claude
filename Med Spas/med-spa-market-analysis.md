# Med Spa Market Analysis: 6-City Overview

**Date:** 2026-03-05 | **Total Med Spas Analyzed:** 4,041

## TL;DR

We scraped and enriched 4,041 med spas across DFW, Houston, NYC, Phoenix, Atlanta, and Charlotte. The market is dominated by Segment 1 (Leaky Funnel) at 74% -- these are high-rated businesses with websites and reviews but leaking conversions. **39% have no online booking** and **29% have zero social media presence**, representing the two biggest actionable gaps. Nearly half (44%) of all 4.0+-rated med spas have weak digital presence (missing booking, social, or low site score). Charlotte has the lowest digital sophistication; Phoenix and DFW are slightly more mature. The sweet spot for outreach: Seg 1 med spas with high ratings, no online booking, and missing social channels -- roughly 900+ businesses across all 6 cities.

## Key Takeaways for Sales Outreach

1. **Target Seg 1 without booking first** -- 919 businesses that are successful but missing a basic conversion tool
2. **Charlotte is the easiest market** -- least sophisticated, smallest (less noise), highest gap rates
3. **"Med spa" is only half the market** -- adapt messaging for skin care clinics, day spas, facial spas, laser clinics
4. **TikTok is wide open** -- under 21% adoption everywhere, easy value-add to pitch
5. **1,186 businesses have ZERO social presence** -- low-hanging fruit for social media management services
6. **NYC has volume but lower quality sites** -- avg site score 6.8 vs 7.3 elsewhere suggests more technical gaps to fix
