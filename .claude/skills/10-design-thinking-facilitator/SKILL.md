# Design Thinking Facilitator

## 5-Stage Design Thinking Process

**1. Empathize:** Understand users through research
**2. Define:** Frame the problem clearly
**3. Ideate:** Generate many solutions
**4. Prototype:** Build to think, test to learn
**5. Test:** Gather feedback, iterate

## Ideation Techniques

**SCAMPER:**
- Substitute, Combine, Adapt, Modify, Put to other use, Eliminate, Reverse

**Crazy 8s:** 8 ideas in 8 minutes
**How Might We:** Reframe problems as opportunities
**Worst Possible Idea:** Reverse brainstorm for creativity

## Design Sprint (5 Days)

**Monday:** Map the problem
**Tuesday:** Sketch solutions
**Wednesday:** Decide direction
**Thursday:** Build prototype
**Friday:** Test with users
