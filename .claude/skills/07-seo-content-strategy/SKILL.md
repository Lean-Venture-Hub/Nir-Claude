# SEO & Content Strategy

Search engine optimization and strategic content planning for organic growth.

## 1. On-Page SEO Checklist

**Title Tag (55-60 characters):** Primary keyword + benefit + brand
**Meta Description (150-160 characters):** Benefit + keywords + CTA  
**URL Structure:** Short, descriptive, keyword-rich, lowercase
**Header Tags:** H1 (one per page) → H2 (main sections) → H3 (subsections)
**Content:** Primary keyword in first 100 words, 1-2% density, LSI keywords

## 2. Keyword Research Process

1. Brainstorm seed keywords
2. Use tools (Google Keyword Planner, Ahrefs, SEMrush)
3. Analyze search volume and competition
4. Identify user intent (informational/navigational/transactional)
5. Select target keywords
6. Map to content

**Keyword Types:**
- Short-tail: High volume, high competition (category pages)
- Long-tail: Lower volume, lower competition, higher intent (blog posts)
- Question keywords: FAQ content, how-to guides

## 3. Content Calendar & Strategy

**Content Pillars Approach:**
```
Pillar Page (comprehensive guide)
├─ Cluster article 1
├─ Cluster article 2  
├─ Cluster article 3
└─ Cluster article 4
```

**Content Mix:** 70% proven performers, 20% trending topics, 10% experiments

## 4. Link Building

**Internal:** 3-5 links per article, descriptive anchor text, topic clusters
**External:** Link to authoritative sources, open in new tab
**Backlinks:** Create linkable assets, guest posting, digital PR, resource pages

## 5. Performance Tracking

**Key Metrics:** Organic traffic, keyword rankings, engagement (bounce rate, time on page), conversions

**Monthly Review:** Traffic trends, top performing content, new rankings, opportunities
