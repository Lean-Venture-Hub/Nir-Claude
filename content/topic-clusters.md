## Cluster 1: Oral Health Education

**SEO keywords to embed:** how to brush teeth correctly, how often to floss, tooth sensitivity causes, how to whiten teeth at home

**Viral hooks:**
- "Most people do this wrong..." (brushing technique)
- "Your dentist won't judge you for this (but you should fix it)"
- "The 30-second habit that saves teeth"

**Content types:**
- Step-by-step tip slides (swipeable)
- Myth vs. truth polls
- "Rate your routine" interactive stories

**Hashtags (mix per post):**
- Broad: #dentalhealth #oralcare #teethtips #dentist
- Niche: #brushingtips #flossing #toothenamel #cavityprevention
- Local: #[city]dentist #[city]dental #[neighborhood]smiles

**Engagement mechanic:** Poll ("Do you floss daily? Yes / I know I should...") — drives saves + replies

## Cluster 2: Smile Transformations

**SEO keywords:** teeth whitening results, Invisalign before after, veneers transformation, dental implant results

**Viral hooks:**
- "6 months of Invisalign at [Clinic Name]" — progress reveal
- "She said yes, and her smile was ready" (wedding season)
- "He hadn't been to the dentist in 8 years. Here's what we found."

**Content types:**
- Split-frame before/after (vertical for Stories)
- Patient journey mini-series (3 posts over 3 weeks)
- Result reveal with text overlay ("Same-day whitening")

**Compliance note:** Always add "Results may vary" in fine print. Never imply guaranteed outcomes.

**Hashtags:**
- #smiletransformation #beforeandafter #teethwhitening #invisalign #veneers #dentalimplants
- Service-specific: #cosmeticdentistry #smilemakeover

**Engagement mechanic:** "Would you do this?" poll or "DM us for pricing" CTA — drives inquiry

**Content types:**

## Cluster 6: Promotions

**SEO keywords:** dentist deals [city], new patient special dental, affordable dentist [city], dental cleaning cost

**Viral hooks:**
- "We're running our once-a-year deal — here's what you get"
- "Refer a friend. You both win." (loss aversion + reciprocity)
- "Your insurance resets in 30 days — don't waste $1,500"

**Content types:**
- Offer card with expiration date
- Insurance benefit reminder (December/January)
- Referral program mechanics (how it works, 3 slides)

**Compliance note:** Never claim "lowest price in [city]" or make comparative pricing claims without evidence.

**Hashtags:**
- #dentaldeals #newpatient #dentalinsurance #affordabledentist #[city]dentist

**Engagement mechanic:** Countdown timer sticker on Stories + "DM to book" CTA = urgency without hard sell

## Cluster 7: Patient Stories & Reviews

**SEO keywords:** [clinic name] reviews, best dentist [city], dentist I trust, dental anxiety help

**Viral hooks:**
- "I hadn't been to the dentist in 6 years. I was terrified. Here's what happened."
- "This patient drove 45 minutes to see us. Here's why."
- "5 stars. Again. Here's what they said."

**Content types:**
- Review card (star rating + pull quote + first name)
- Before/after with patient-written caption
- Anxiety story ("I used to panic — now I look forward to cleanings")

**HIPAA guardrails:**
- First name only (or "a patient shared...")
- Written consent for photos and detailed stories
- Never include identifiable medical info (procedure type) without consent

**Hashtags:**
- #patientreviews #5stars #trusteddentist #dentalanxiety #smilewithconfidence #[city]dentist

**Engagement mechanic:** "Tag someone who's been putting off their appointment" — organic reach multiplier

## Cross-Cluster Content Rules

| Rule | Rationale |
|------|-----------|
| Max 2 promotional posts per week | Avoid "salesy" feed fatigue |
| Min 1 educational post per day | Builds follow-worthy authority |
| Min 1 engagement mechanic per day | Poll, quiz, CTA, or tag |
| Seasonal content starts 10 days early | Captures early search intent |
| Every 4th post should be shareable | Design for save + forward |
| Local hashtag on every post | Drives local discovery |
| No medical claims — ever | Compliance + trust |
