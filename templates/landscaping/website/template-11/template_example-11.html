Our Story

A Family Business,
Through & Through

I'm Mike Anderson, and I started GreenScape Pro in 2011 with a beat-up truck and a belief that every family deserves a yard they actually want to spend time in.

What began as weekend side projects for neighbors has grown into Austin's most trusted landscaping team — but we've never lost that neighborly spirit. We still answer our own phones, and I'm still on-site for every project kickoff.

"We don't just plant gardens — we plant the roots for family memories."
