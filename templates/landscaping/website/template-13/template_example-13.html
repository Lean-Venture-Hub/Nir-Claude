Our Story

Rooted in Passion

Founded in 2011 by Mike Anderson, GreenScape Pro has grown from a small Austin landscaping crew into the city's most sought-after landscape design firm.

With over 1,200 gardens transformed, our team combines horticultural expertise with architectural vision to create outdoor spaces that feel like private botanical gardens.

"Every garden tells a story. Our role is to help nature write its most beautiful chapter on your land."

— Mike Anderson, Founder
