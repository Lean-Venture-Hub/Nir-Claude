Our Story
"Every landscape tells a story. Our job is to make yours unforgettable."

Founded in 2011 by Mike Anderson, GreenScape Pro began with a simple belief: outdoor spaces should be as thoughtfully designed as the homes they surround.

After fifteen years of transforming Austin properties — from intimate courtyard gardens to sprawling estate landscapes — we've built a reputation for uncompromising quality and design-forward thinking. Every project starts with listening, and ends with something extraordinary.

Our team of certified landscape architects, master craftsmen, and horticultural specialists brings a rare combination of artistic vision and technical precision to every project we touch.

How We Work

From Vision to Reality

Step 01

Discovery & Design

We begin with a thorough site assessment and in-depth consultation. Understanding your vision, lifestyle, and budget allows us to create a comprehensive design plan with 3D renderings, material selections, and detailed specifications.

Step 02

Build & Transform

Our expert crews bring the design to life with meticulous craftsmanship. We manage every detail — from grading and drainage to plant installation and hardscape construction — keeping you informed at every milestone.

Step 03

Maintain & Evolve

Your landscape is a living investment. We offer tailored maintenance programs that ensure your outdoor space matures beautifully — seasonal planting, irrigation management, pruning, and proactive care that protects your investment for decades.
