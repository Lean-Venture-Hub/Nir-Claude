The Story

Mike Anderson

Founder & Lead Designer — Since 2011
With over fourteen years transforming Austin's outdoor spaces, Mike Anderson founded GreenScape Pro on a simple belief: that landscape design is an art form deserving the same intention and craft as architecture. What began as a one-man operation with a pickup truck and a sketchbook has evolved into one of Austin's most respected landscape studios. Mike's approach blends horticultural science with an artist's eye — every project starts with understanding the land, the light, and the lives that will unfold within the space. A graduate of Texas A&M's Horticulture program and a certified landscape designer, Mike leads a team of twelve dedicated professionals who share his commitment to creating landscapes that are as sustainable as they are stunning. The studio has completed over 1,200 projects across the greater Austin area, from intimate courtyard gardens to sprawling estate landscapes, earning a 4.9-star rating from 312 satisfied clients.
"Every landscape tells a story. Our job is to make sure it's a beautiful one — one that grows richer with every passing season."

How We Work

Our Design Process

01.

Discovery & Vision

Every project begins with a conversation — not about plants or pavers, but about how you live. We visit your property, study the light, the soil, the existing trees, and the natural contours of the land. From this understanding, we develop a design vision that's uniquely yours. This isn't a template; it's a bespoke composition crafted for your specific landscape and lifestyle.
02.

Design & Presentation

Our design team creates detailed plans including 3D renderings, plant palettes, material samples, and phased timelines. You'll see exactly how your space will look before a single stone is laid. We present two to three concept directions and refine your favorite until every detail feels right. Transparent pricing accompanies each option — no surprises, just informed decisions.
03.

Craftsmanship & Reveal

Installation is where vision becomes reality. Our crews work with meticulous attention to detail, treating every joint, every grade, and every planting as if it were their own home. Upon completion, we walk through together. We explain care instructions for every plant, demonstrate your irrigation system, and schedule seasonal check-ins to ensure your landscape matures beautifully.
