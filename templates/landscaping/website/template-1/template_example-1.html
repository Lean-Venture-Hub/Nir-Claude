Our Story

Rooted in Austin,
Growing Together

When Mike Anderson started GreenScape Pro in 2011 with a single truck and a passion for turning ordinary yards into extraordinary retreats, he had one rule: treat every property like it was his own.

Fifteen years and 2,400+ projects later, that philosophy hasn't changed. Our crew of 22 certified landscapers, designers, and horticulturists are all Austin locals who understand the unique challenges of Central Texas landscaping — from the limestone soil to the summer heat.

We're not just landscapers. We're your neighbors, invested in making Austin more beautiful one yard at a time.
