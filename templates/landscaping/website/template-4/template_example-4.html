Our Story

From a One-Truck Crew to Austin's Most Trusted Team

When Mike Anderson started GreenScape Pro in 2011, he had one truck, a handful of tools, and a vision: bring design-forward landscaping to every home in Austin — not just the mansions.

Today, our team of 24 landscape professionals has completed over 1,800 projects across Central Texas. We still operate with the same hands-on attention that built our reputation. Every project starts with Mike on-site, and every detail gets his sign-off.

— Mike Anderson, Founder
