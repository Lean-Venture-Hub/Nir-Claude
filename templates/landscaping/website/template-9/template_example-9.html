Our Story

Rooted in
Austin Soil

Mike Anderson founded GreenScape Pro in 2011 with a belief that great landscapes aren't imposed on the land — they're drawn from it. What started as a one-person operation has grown into a team of 18 dedicated craftspeople who share a reverence for outdoor spaces.

Every project begins with listening: to the land, to the light, and to how you want to feel when you step outside. We don't rush. The best landscapes, like the best things in life, take time.

"A garden should feel like it grew there on its own. Our job is simply to guide it."

How We Work

Three Deliberate Steps

01

Listen & Observe

We visit your property, study the light, soil, and existing elements. Then we listen to your vision and how you want to experience your outdoor space.

02

Design & Refine

Our design process is iterative and collaborative. We present concepts, gather your feedback, and refine until every detail feels right.

03

Craft & Cultivate

Installation is handled by our own crew — no subcontractors. We stay on-site until the last stone is placed and the last plant is settled in.
