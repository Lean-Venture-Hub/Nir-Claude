Our Story

Built on Craftsmanship

Founded in 2006, Precision Auto Works has grown from a two-bay garage into Dallas's most trusted auto repair shop. Owner Mike Rodriguez and his team of ASE-certified technicians have completed over 50,000 repairs with a 4.9-star rating. We believe in transparent pricing, honest diagnostics, and treating every car like our own.

Every vehicle that enters our shop receives the same meticulous attention — from routine oil changes to complex engine rebuilds. Our team of ASE-certified technicians combines decades of hands-on experience with cutting-edge diagnostic technology.

What We Do

Expert Services

Oil Changes & Fluid Services

Full-synthetic and conventional oil changes, transmission fluid, coolant flushes, and brake fluid service. We use premium filters and fluids to keep your engine running at peak performance.

Brake Repair & Replacement

Complete brake system service including pads, rotors, calipers, and brake lines. We ensure your stopping power is reliable and safe for Dallas roads.

Engine Diagnostics & Repair

Advanced computer diagnostics to pinpoint check engine lights, misfires, and performance issues. Our ASE-certified technicians handle everything from tune-ups to major engine work.

Tire Services & Alignment

Tire mounting, balancing, rotation, and precision four-wheel alignment. We carry all major tire brands and help you find the right fit for your vehicle and budget.

AC & Heating Service

Stay comfortable year-round with expert climate control service. From refrigerant recharges to compressor replacement, we keep your cabin at the perfect temperature.

Transmission Service

Transmission fluid changes, filter replacement, and complete transmission repair for both automatic and manual vehicles. Preventive maintenance that saves you thousands.
