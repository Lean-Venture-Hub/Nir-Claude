About

Where Craft Meets
Automotive Expertise

Founded by Mike Thompson in 2008, Precision Auto Care was built on a simple conviction: that every vehicle deserves the same meticulous attention you'd expect from a European specialist.

Our Austin facility houses factory-grade diagnostics and a team of ASE-certified technicians who treat every engine like their own. From routine maintenance to complex repairs, we deliver transparency, precision, and care.
