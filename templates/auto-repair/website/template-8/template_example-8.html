About

Precision Auto Care was founded by Mike Thompson in 2008 with a simple promise: fix it right, explain it clearly, and charge fairly. Today, our team of ASE-certified technicians serves Austin from a 6-bay facility equipped with the latest diagnostic technology. We believe every customer deserves transparency — you'll always know what's wrong, what it costs, and why.
