What We Do

Professional auto repair services with state-of-the-art equipment and ASE-certified technicians.

Oil Change & Lube

Quick and thorough oil changes using premium synthetic and conventional oils. We inspect all fluid levels and filters to keep your engine running smooth.

Brake Service

Complete brake inspections, pad and rotor replacements, brake fluid flushes, and ABS diagnostics. Your safety is our top priority.

Engine Repair

From minor tune-ups to major overhauls, our master technicians diagnose and repair all engine issues with precision and care.

Transmission

Transmission fluid services, diagnostics, rebuilds, and replacements for both automatic and manual transmissions.

Tire Service

Tire rotations, balancing, alignments, and new tire installation. We carry all major brands and offer competitive pricing.

Diagnostics

Advanced computer diagnostics to pinpoint issues fast. We use OEM-level scan tools to read codes and identify problems accurately.

Precision Auto Care has been Austin's trusted neighborhood mechanic since 2008. Founded by Mike Thompson, an ASE Master Technician with over 20 years of experience, we believe every driver deserves honest, transparent auto repair.
