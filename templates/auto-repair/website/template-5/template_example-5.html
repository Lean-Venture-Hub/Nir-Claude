About

A Passion for European Machines

Our Story

Precision Auto Care has been Austin's trusted European auto specialist since 2008. Founded by Mike Thompson, an ASE Master Technician with over 20 years of experience working exclusively with European vehicles, we bring dealer-level expertise at independent shop prices.

What started as a passion for German engineering has grown into Austin's premier European auto specialty shop — equipped with the latest factory diagnostic tools and staffed by technicians who live and breathe these vehicles.

Every car that comes through our doors receives personalized attention. We know these machines inside and out, and we give them the care they deserve.
