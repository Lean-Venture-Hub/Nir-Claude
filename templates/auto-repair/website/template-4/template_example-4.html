About Precision

A Mechanic You Can Count On

Precision Auto Care has been Austin's trusted neighborhood mechanic since 2008. Founded by Mike Thompson, an ASE Master Technician with over 20 years of experience, we believe every driver deserves honest, transparent auto repair. Our team of certified technicians treats every vehicle like their own.
