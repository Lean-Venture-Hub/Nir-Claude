About Us

Meet Mike Thompson

ASE Master Tech, 20+ years

When I opened Precision Auto Care in 2008, I made one promise: we'll never recommend a repair you don't need. Eighteen years later, that promise hasn't changed. We're a family-owned shop that treats every customer like a neighbor — because most of you are.

Our team of ASE-certified technicians combines decades of experience with a genuine commitment to doing things right. We photograph every issue before we touch it, explain everything in plain English, and stand behind our work with a 3-year warranty.
