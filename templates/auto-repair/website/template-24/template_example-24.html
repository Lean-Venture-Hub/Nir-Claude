A Legacy of Honest Auto Care

When Mike Rodriguez opened the doors of Precision Auto Works in 2006, the mission was simple: treat every car like it was family, and every customer like a neighbor. Nearly two decades later, that handshake promise still drives everything we do.

From a two-bay shop to a full-service garage trusted by hundreds of families across Dallas, our story is one of dedication, integrity, and a genuine love for the craft of automotive repair.

What We Do

Expert Services

Oil Changes & Fluid Services

Conventional, synthetic, and high-mileage oil changes plus complete fluid system maintenance to keep your engine running at peak performance.

Brake Repair & Replacement

Comprehensive brake inspections, pad and rotor replacement, brake fluid flushes, and complete brake system diagnostics for safe stopping power.

Engine Diagnostics & Repair

Advanced computer diagnostics, check engine light diagnosis, engine rebuilds, and precision repairs using factory-grade scan tools.

Tire Services & Alignment

Tire mounting, balancing, rotation, flat repair, and precision 4-wheel alignment to extend tire life and improve handling.

AC & Heating Service

Complete climate control service including AC recharge, compressor repair, heater core replacement, and full system diagnostics.

Transmission Service

Transmission fluid changes, filter replacement, diagnostics, and repair for both automatic and manual transmissions.

Our Story

Meet Mike Rodriguez

Founded in 2006, Precision Auto Works has grown from a two-bay garage into Dallas's most trusted auto repair shop. Owner Mike Rodriguez and his team of ASE-certified technicians have completed over 50,000 repairs with a 4.9-star rating. We believe in transparent pricing, honest diagnostics, and treating every car like our own.

With ASE-certified technicians and state-of-the-art diagnostic equipment, we bridge old-school values with modern expertise. Every vehicle gets a thorough inspection, honest assessment, and quality repairs backed by our warranty.

"We don't just fix cars — we build relationships. When you bring your car here, you're family."

— Mike Rodriguez, Founder
