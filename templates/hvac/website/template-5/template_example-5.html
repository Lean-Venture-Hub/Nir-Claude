About ClimatePro

Built on Family Values & American Work Ethic

When Mike Thompson founded ClimatePro Heating & Cooling in 2008, he had one simple promise: treat every home like it's your own. That principle still drives us today.

As a family-owned business rooted in the Dallas community, we believe in honest estimates, showing up on time, and doing the job right the first time. Our NATE-certified technicians are your neighbors — they live here, work here, and take pride in keeping this community comfortable.

We're proud to offer military discounts and first-responder appreciation because we believe in taking care of those who take care of us.
