The Story

A Legacy of Comfort

"Every home deserves a climate system that works silently and perfectly — that's the standard we set for every single installation."

When Mike Thompson founded ClimatePro in 2008, he had one vision: bring honesty and technical excellence back to the HVAC industry. What started as a two-person operation in a Dallas garage has grown into one of the most trusted climate service teams in North Texas.

Today, our NATE-certified team of 28 technicians serves over 4,000 homes and businesses across the DFW metroplex, with a 4.9-star rating that reflects our unwavering commitment to getting every detail right.
