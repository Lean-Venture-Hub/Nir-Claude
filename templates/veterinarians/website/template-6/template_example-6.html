About PawCare

Rooted in Compassion, Growing with Care

Founded in 2015 by Dr. Sarah Mitchell, PawCare Veterinary Clinic was born from a simple belief: veterinary care should nurture the whole animal — body, mind, and spirit.

Our wellness-centered approach combines modern veterinary medicine with holistic practices, creating a warm, stress-free environment where pets and their families feel genuinely cared for. Nestled in the heart of Denver, we've grown from a small practice into a trusted community partner.
