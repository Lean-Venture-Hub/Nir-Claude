Our Story

A Practice Built
on Compassion

When Dr. Sarah Mitchell founded PawCare in 2015, she had a simple vision: create a veterinary clinic where pets feel safe, owners feel heard, and medicine is practiced with both rigor and heart.

Today, that vision has grown into a team of dedicated professionals who share Dr. Mitchell's commitment to excellence. Every member of our staff — from veterinarians to veterinary technicians — is chosen not just for their expertise, but for their genuine love of animals.

"We don't just treat patients — we become part of their families. That relationship is the foundation of everything we do."

How It Works

Your First Visit,
Simplified

We've designed every step of the experience to be as stress-free for you and your pet as possible.

Schedule

Call us or book online. We'll find a time that works for your schedule and send you new patient forms to complete at your convenience — no clipboard in the waiting room.

Meet & Examine

Dr. Mitchell takes her time. Every visit includes a thorough examination, honest conversation about findings, and a clear explanation of any recommended care — no pressure, ever.

Ongoing Care

You'll leave with a personalized care plan and direct access to our team for follow-up questions. We schedule reminders so you never miss an important wellness milestone.
