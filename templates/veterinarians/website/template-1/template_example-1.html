About Us

A Family Clinic Built on Compassion

Founded by Dr. Sarah Mitchell in 2015, PawCare was born from a simple belief: every pet deserves the same quality of care we'd give our own.

What started as a small neighborhood practice has grown into Denver's most trusted veterinary clinic. Dr. Mitchell and our team of dedicated professionals combine cutting-edge medicine with genuine warmth and compassion.

We know your pet is family. That's why we take the time to listen, explain, and partner with you in every healthcare decision. From puppy's first visit to senior wellness, we're here for every wag, purr, and milestone.
