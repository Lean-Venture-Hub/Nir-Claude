About PawCare

Where Passion Meets Precision

Founded in 2015 by Dr. Sarah Mitchell, PawCare Veterinary Clinic was built on a simple belief: every pet deserves access to modern, compassionate medicine — delivered by a team that treats them like family.

Our Denver clinic combines state-of-the-art diagnostic technology with a warm, welcoming environment. From routine wellness exams to complex surgical procedures, we bring the same level of dedication and expertise to every visit.
