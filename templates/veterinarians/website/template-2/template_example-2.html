About Us

Where Modern Medicine Meets Genuine Compassion

Founded in 2015, PawCare Veterinary Clinic was born from Dr. Sarah Mitchell's vision of a practice that combines cutting-edge veterinary medicine with the warmth and attention every pet owner deserves.

Dr. Mitchell earned her DVM from Colorado State University and completed advanced training in internal medicine and emergency care. With a team of 8 dedicated professionals, we've cared for over 15,000 patients across the Denver metro area.

Our 5,000 sq. ft. facility features digital radiography, in-house laboratory, ultrasound, and a fully equipped surgical suite — ensuring your pet receives hospital-level care in a comfortable, fear-free environment.
