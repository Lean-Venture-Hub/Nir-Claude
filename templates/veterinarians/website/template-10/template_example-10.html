Our Story
"Every pet deserves gentle hands and a warm heart."

Meet Dr. Sarah Mitchell

When Dr. Mitchell opened PawCare Veterinary Clinic in 2015, she had a simple dream: to create a place where animals feel safe and owners feel heard. Inspired by her childhood on a Colorado ranch, she designed a clinic that feels more like a cozy farmhouse than a sterile office.

Today, our team of passionate veterinarians and technicians continues that tradition — blending modern medicine with old-fashioned compassion. From nervous puppies to senior cats, every patient gets the unhurried, personal attention they deserve.
