About Us

Caring for Pets Like
They're Our Own

Founded by Dr. Sarah Mitchell in 2015, PawCare Veterinary Clinic was built on a simple philosophy: every pet deserves gentle, thorough, and compassionate medical care.

Our state-of-the-art facility in Denver is equipped with advanced diagnostic tools and staffed by a team that genuinely loves animals. We take the time to listen, explain, and create treatment plans that work for both pets and their families.
