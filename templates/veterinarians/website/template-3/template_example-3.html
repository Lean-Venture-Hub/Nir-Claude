Our Story

Meet Dr. Sarah Mitchell & Team

Founded in 2015 with a simple belief: every pet deserves the same quality of care we'd give our own. Dr. Mitchell and her team of 12 passionate professionals bring warmth, expertise, and a whole lot of belly rubs to every visit.

From golden retrievers to grumpy cats, anxious rabbits to adventurous ferrets — we treat every patient like family. Our clinic features state-of-the-art diagnostic equipment, a dedicated surgical suite, and cozy recovery rooms.
