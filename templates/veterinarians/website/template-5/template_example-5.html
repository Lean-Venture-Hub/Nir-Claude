Our Story

A Legacy of
Compassionate Care

Founded in 2015, PawCare Veterinary Clinic was born from Dr. Sarah Mitchell's vision of creating a veterinary practice that combines cutting-edge medicine with genuine warmth and compassion for every patient who walks through our doors.

With a team of board-certified specialists, licensed veterinary technicians, and a deep commitment to continuing education, we've built Denver's most trusted destination for pet health. Our state-of-the-art facility features advanced diagnostic imaging, a fully equipped surgical suite, and a fear-free certified environment.

Dr. Mitchell holds advanced certifications in internal medicine and soft tissue surgery, bringing over 15 years of clinical experience to every consultation.
