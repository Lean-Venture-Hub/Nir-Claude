About PawCare

Built for Critical Moments

Founded in 2015 by Dr. Sarah Mitchell, PawCare Veterinary Clinic was born from a simple belief: no pet owner should ever be turned away during an emergency. After seeing too many families with nowhere to go at 2 AM, she built Denver's most trusted 24/7 veterinary emergency center.

Today, our team of board-certified veterinarians and emergency technicians has treated over 15,000 animals — from routine wellness visits to complex life-saving surgeries. With a 4.8-star rating from 287 reviews, our commitment to excellence speaks for itself.
