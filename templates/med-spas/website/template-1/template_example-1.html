Meet Your Provider

Dr. Elena Vasquez

With over 15 years of experience in aesthetic medicine, Dr. Vasquez founded Radiance MedSpa in 2018 with a simple philosophy: every treatment should enhance your natural beauty, never mask it.

Her approach blends medical precision with an artist's sensibility — listening carefully to your goals, then crafting a personalized plan that delivers subtle, stunning results. Patients describe her touch as "effortlessly natural."

Dr. Vasquez holds board certifications in both Dermatology and Aesthetic Medicine, and regularly trains with world-renowned specialists to bring the latest techniques to Scottsdale.
