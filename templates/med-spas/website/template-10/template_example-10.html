About Radiance

Meet Dr. Elena Vasquez

Board-certified and fellowship-trained in aesthetic medicine, Dr. Elena Vasquez founded Radiance MedSpa & Aesthetics in 2018 with a singular vision: to bring Scottsdale a medical spa that combines clinical precision with an unforgettable luxury experience.

With over a decade of experience and advanced training in injectables, laser technology, and regenerative aesthetics, Dr. Vasquez personally oversees every treatment plan. Her approach blends artistry with science — because your confidence deserves nothing less.
