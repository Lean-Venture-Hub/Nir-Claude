Our Story

Where Science Meets
Editorial Beauty

Founded in 2018 by Dr. Elena Vasquez, Radiance MedSpa & Aesthetics was born from a belief that aesthetic medicine should feel less clinical and more like a curated experience — intimate, personalized, and transformative.

Board-certified in aesthetic medicine with over 15 years of experience, Dr. Vasquez has assembled a team of master injectors, licensed aestheticians, and wellness specialists who share her philosophy: that true beauty is an enhancement of what already exists.
