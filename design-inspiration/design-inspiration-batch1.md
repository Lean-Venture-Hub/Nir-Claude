## So What?

For building client websites (auto repair, dental, med spa, landscaping), the direct application is not copying these styles wholesale — most of these are for tech products and agencies. The relevant takeaways:

1. **Scroll animation is table stakes** — even local business sites benefit from Lenis + simple GSAP reveals. Clients notice the polish.
2. **Dark palette = premium signal** — med spa and high-end dental clients can use dark themes to signal luxury vs. the clinical white everyone defaults to.
3. **Typography-first layout** — oversized location/headline type is immediately differentiating in local business verticals where every competitor uses the same template.
4. **Micro-interactions on CTAs** — hover states on phone numbers, booking buttons, and nav items are essentially free perceived quality upgrades.
5. **Bento grid for services/features** — Amie and Notion prove this works. Directly applicable to "our services" sections.
