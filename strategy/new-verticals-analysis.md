# New Verticals Analysis — Website + VA Pipeline
_Updated: 2026-03-14 (with consumer discovery research)_

## TL;DR — Revised Top 5

| Rank | Vertical | Score /50 | Key Insight from Research |
|------|----------|-----------|--------------------------|
| 1 | **Plumbers** | 48 | 84% start on Google, 59% of sites aren't mobile-optimized, $55-120 CPL — our website is instant ROI |

**Biggest change:** Chiropractors dropped from #3 to #5. Research shows ~90% already have websites — the "here's your site" cold pitch won't land because they already have one. Roofers jumped up because 33% have no site and the trust-validation dynamic is perfect for our model.

## Revised Outreach Strategy by Vertical

### Plumbers & Electricians (batch 1)
- **Pitch angle:** "Your site doesn't work on phones — 59% of plumber sites don't. Here's one that does."
- **Lead finding:** Google Maps → filter by 4+ stars, 50+ reviews, check if site exists/is mobile
- **Key stat to cite in outreach:** "84% of people Google before calling a plumber. 40% won't hire you without a professional site."
- **VA upsell hook:** "You're losing emergency calls at 2am — our AI picks up every time"

### Roofers (batch 2)
- **Pitch angle:** "79% of your customers get your name from a neighbor. Then 91% Google you to check. If your site looks bad — or you don't have one — you lose the job."
- **Lead finding:** Google Maps → filter by reviews, verify no site or bad site
- **Key stat:** "Roofers with a simple website close 2x more jobs than those without"
- **VA upsell hook:** "After a storm, 50 people call at once — our AI gives every caller a quote and schedules an inspection"

### Cleaning Services (batch 3)
- **Pitch angle:** "Stop giving Thumbtack 25% of every job. Get your own site, own your customers."
- **Lead finding:** Google Maps + Nextdoor mentions, look for businesses on Thumbtack without own site
- **Key stat:** "Businesses with their own booking site convert 2-3x more leads than marketplace-only businesses"
- **VA upsell hook:** "Recurring scheduling, rebooking reminders, and quote requests — all handled automatically"

### Chiropractors (deprioritized)
- **IF we pursue:** Shift from "here's your site" to "your site only converts 3-5% — here's one that converts 3x better"
- **Risk:** Harder sell, requires proving our site is better than theirs
- **When:** Only after proving model works with trades

## Revised Launch Order

1. **Plumbers + Electricians** — highest score, emergency urgency, high CPL makes our $100/mo trivial. Launch together as "trades" batch
2. **Roofers** — build before storm season. Trust-validation loop is our strongest pitch angle
3. **Cleaning Services** — marketplace escape pitch + recurring model validates VA upsell
4. **Pest Control** (moved up from lower list) — seasonal, recurring, fragmented, similar profile to cleaning
5. **Chiropractors** (deprioritized) — only if we develop a "replacement" pitch for businesses that already have sites

Each vertical: build 8-10 templates, generate 20 demo sites from Google Maps leads, run outreach, measure reply rate before scaling.
