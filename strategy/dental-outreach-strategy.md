# Dental Clinic Cold Outreach Strategy — Gush Dan, Israel
**Date:** 2026-02-23 | **Market:** 266 clinics, 5 segments

---

## S2: Warm Digital, No Proof (75 clinics) — STRIKE FIRST

**Profile:** Has website, 4.0+ rating, <20 reviews. Already invested. Just leaking.

| Element | What to Do |
|---|---|
| **Pre-ask gift** | "Your Patient Voice Report" — scrape all their existing Google + Facebook reviews, cluster them by theme, show the top 3 complaints and top 3 praises in a 1-page PDF. Add: "You're leaving ~40 new reviews/month on the table based on your patient volume." |
| **Hook/Subject** | "מצאנו 6 דברים שהמטופלים שלך אומרים — אבל אתה לא שומע" |
| **Delivery** | WhatsApp voice note (30 sec) from a real person + PDF link. Voice note = "שלחנו לך משהו שחשבנו שיעניין אותך לגבי המרפאה..." |
| **Emotional trigger** | Fear of blind spots. You think you're doing great — but patients are talking, and you don't know what they're saying. |
| **CTA** | "רוצה לראות את הדוח המלא עם כל הביקורות? 10 דקות בזום ואני מסביר." |

**Why this works:** They're warm. They care about digital. The report is 100% personalized to THEIR reviews. The ask is tiny.

## S1: Leaky Funnel (20 clinics)

**Profile:** Good site (5+), 4.0+ rating, 20–29 reviews. Almost there. Just slow.

| Element | What to Do |
|---|---|
| **Pre-ask gift** | "Funnel Leak Audit" — show their Google Business profile vs. 3 direct competitors within 500m. Side-by-side: review count, rating, site score, booking UX. Visualized as a simple comparison card. |
| **Hook/Subject** | "המרפאה ברחוב הסמוך עוקפת אתכם — הנה ההוכחה" |
| **Delivery** | Email with a personal loom video (2 min) walking through the comparison. Subject line creates urgency without being spammy. |
| **Emotional trigger** | Competitive loss aversion. No one wants to lose to the clinic down the street. |
| **CTA** | "אני יכול לשלוח לך את כל הנתונים על המתחרים שלך — רלוונטי?" |

**Why this works:** Small segment = high-touch is viable. The competitor card is sticky — they'll show it to their staff.

## S3: Reputation Rescue (44 clinics) — MOST URGENT EMOTIONAL BUY

**Profile:** Rating <4.0, <15 reviews. Reputation is damaged. This is existential for them.

| Element | What to Do |
|---|---|
| **Pre-ask gift** | "Reputation Risk Report" — pull every negative review they have, categorize by complaint type (wait time, price, pain, communication), AND show them the exact keywords patients are searching to find them. Add: "כל יום שעובר, מטופלים חדשים רואים את הציון הזה ויוצאים." |
| **Hook/Subject** | "ציון 3.8 על גוגל עולה לך [X] מטופלים בחודש — חישבנו" |
| **Delivery** | Physical letter to the clinic (sealed, marked "לרופא/ה בלבד") + follow-up WhatsApp 48h later. Physical = stands out, signals seriousness. |
| **Emotional trigger** | Shame + loss + urgency. This is their livelihood. A bad rating is a slow bleed. |
| **CTA** | "יש לנו תוכנית שהצליחה להעלות ציון מ-3.6 ל-4.3 תוך 60 יום. רוצה לשמוע?" |

**Why this works:** The envelope gets opened. The personalized loss calculation (patients/month) is a punch in the gut — in the best way.

## S4: Invisible Good Clinics (105 clinics) — BIGGEST VOLUME OPPORTUNITY

**Profile:** Rating 4.5+, site_score 0–3. Great service, zero digital presence. Bat Yam = 38 clinics.

| Element | What to Do |
|---|---|
| **Pre-ask gift** | Mock-up landing page — build a LIVE, functioning 1-pager for their clinic (using their name, rating, address, Google photo) at a temp URL. Show them: "זה מה שהאתר שלך יכול להיראות — בנינו אותו בשבילך, בחינם." |
| **Hook/Subject** | "בנינו לך אתר — בוא לראות אותו לפני שמוחקים אותו" |
| **Delivery** | WhatsApp with a screenshot of the mock-up + the URL. The visual hook does the work. |
| **Emotional trigger** | Curiosity + tangible proof of capability. They can't unsee what their clinic COULD look like. |
| **CTA** | "אם אתה אוהב את זה, אנחנו יכולים להפוך אותו לאמיתי תוך שבוע. רוצה לדבר?" |

**Bat Yam cluster play:** Run a geo-targeted campaign — "אנחנו בונים נוכחות דיגיטלית לרופאי שיניים בבת ים — כבר עבדנו עם [X] מרפאות ברחוב שלך."

**Why this works:** The mock-up is the proof. You're not TELLING them you can build — you SHOW them. Scarcity framing ("לפני שמוחקים אותו") creates urgency without lying.

## S5: Digitally Absent (22 clinics) — LOWEST PRIORITY, DIFFERENT APPROACH

**Profile:** No website, no social, rating 4.0–4.49. Likely older, skeptical.

| Element | What to Do |
|---|---|
| **Pre-ask gift** | Skip the digital report. Instead: printed one-pager showing "כמה אנשים חיפשו 'רופא שיניים + [העיר שלך]' החודש" with their name on it. Real search volume data. Tactile, not digital. |
| **Hook/Subject** | Walk-in or phone call. No email — they're not there. |
| **Delivery** | In-person drop-in. Receptionist handoff. Brief, warm, Israeli. "אנחנו עובדים עם מרפאות שיניים באזור — הבאנו משהו קטן לרופא." |
| **Emotional trigger** | Missed opportunity, not fear. Frame as: patients are already looking for them — they just can't find them. |
| **CTA** | "אנחנו נותנים חינם חשיפת גוגל לחודש אחד — בלי כסף, בלי מחויבות. רוצים לנסות?" |

**Why this works:** These clinics don't respond to digital. Meet them where they are. The free month removes all friction.

## Shared Principles Across All Segments

- **Gift before ask** — always. The artifact IS the pitch.
- **Personalization signal** — every message must contain 1 piece of data only THEY would know you researched.
- **One ask only** — never ask for a sale in message 1. Ask for 10 minutes.
- **Hebrew** — always. WhatsApp voice note especially. Don't send English to Israeli dentists.
- **Timing** — send Tuesday–Thursday, 9–11am. Avoid Monday (chaotic) and Friday (Shabbat prep).
