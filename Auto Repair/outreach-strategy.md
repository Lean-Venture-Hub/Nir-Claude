# Auto Repair Outreach Strategy
**Date:** 2026-03-14 | **Leads:** 444 S4 (no website) across Houston + Phoenix

## TL;DR
Focus on S4b shops with email first (155 leads). Lead with their own reviews as social proof — "your customers already trust you, let's make it findable." Target is 3-5 clients in 30 days at $500-800 one-time + $99/mo recurring. Do not pitch websites. Pitch more customers finding them.

---

## 1. Priority Segments (who first)

| Priority | Segment | Size | Why |
|----------|---------|------|-----|
| 1 | S4b + has email | ~155 | Established (avg 79 reviews), contactable, proven business |
| 2 | S4b + Facebook only | ~90 | On social, no site — obvious gap they already feel |
| 3 | S4b + Instagram | ~40 | More digitally aware, higher receptivity |
| 4 | S4b + no social at all | ~97 | Phone only — cold call or skip for now |
| 5 | S4a (any contact) | ~50 | Too new, low proof, harder sale — wave 2 |

**Kill:** S4a + no social (12 leads). Not worth the effort. S4a in general is wave 2.

## 3. The Hook

**What NOT to say:** "You need a website." (They've heard it. They don't care. They're busy fixing cars.)

**What WORKS — 3 proven angles:**

| Angle | Hook Line | Why It Works |
|-------|-----------|-------------|
| Trust mirror | "Your customers call you the most honest mechanic in [city]" | Mirrors their own review language. Instant credibility. |
| Invisible problem | "Right now, when someone Googles '[service] near me' in [neighborhood], they can't find you" | Concrete, local, not abstract |
| Competitor gap | "3 shops near you have websites. You have better reviews than all of them." | Uses our data as leverage. Creates urgency without pressure. |

**Master hook (email subject line):** "Your Google reviews are great — here's what's missing"

## 5. Offer Structure

### Pricing Tiers

| Tier | Name | Price | What's Included | Best For |
|------|------|-------|----------------|---------|
| Starter | "Get Found" | $499 one-time + $79/mo | 1-page site, Google Business optimization, contact form, embedded reviews | S4b shops, trust-first |
| Standard | "Get Found + Booked" | $799 one-time + $129/mo | Above + appointment widget, 5 pages, review management dashboard | S4b with 50+ reviews |
| Premium | "Full Digital Presence" | $1,499 one-time + $199/mo | Above + AI assistant (call/chat), social media templates, monthly report | Best shops, long-term |

### Pricing Psychology
- Lead with Standard as the default — it's the anchor
- Starter exists to close price-sensitive shops — do not lead with it
- Premium exists to make Standard look reasonable — mention it once, don't push it
- One-time fee removes the "recurring cost" objection; monthly is framed as "hosting + support"

### Free Hook (optional)
Offer a **free competitor audit** — "Here's how 3 shops near you are showing up vs. you." Takes 5 min to generate from our data. Zero cost. Opens the conversation.

## 6. Using the Data as Leverage

| Data Point We Have | How to Use It |
|-------------------|--------------|
| Their real review quotes | Paste 1-2 into the email — "One of your customers said: '...'" Instantly personal, not generic |
| Competitor website status | "3 shops within 2 miles have websites. You have better ratings than 2 of them." |
| Review count | Social proof of business quality: "With 87 Google reviews at 4.7 stars, you're already one of the best in your area" |
| Missing search presence | "Right now, someone Googling 'brake repair in [neighborhood]' won't find you" |
| Facebook presence | "You're on Facebook but when someone Googles you, nothing comes up" |

**Personalization minimum:** Name, city, review count, 1 actual review quote. Anything less is spam.

## Kill List

- Do not pitch "websites" — pitch "customers finding you"
- Do not send batch emails without personalized review quote — it reads as spam
- Do not lead with pricing — earn the conversation first
- Do not target S4a before validating conversion on S4b
- Do not call shops that have no email AND no social AND less than 20 reviews — wrong ICP

## North Star Metric

**3 paying clients in 30 days.** Everything else is noise until that's hit.
